Catalyst

A Lower Limb Prosthetic Guide

Catalyst is an educational mobile application designed to support lower-limb prosthetic users, caregivers, and clinicians by providing general information about prosthetic use, exercises, common troubleshooting topics, and peer support resources.

Educational use only — not medical advice

Catalyst is intended for educational and informational purposes only and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a licensed healthcare professional for medical decisions.
