Catalyst

A Lower Limb Prosthetic Guide

Catalyst is an educational mobile application designed to support lower-limb prosthetic users, caregivers, and clinicians by providing general information about prosthetic use, exercises, and common troubleshooting topics.

Catalyst is intended for educational purposes only and does not provide medical advice.
