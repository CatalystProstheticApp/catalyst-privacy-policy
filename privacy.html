Privacy Policy – Catalyst

Last updated: February 17, 2026

Catalyst: Prosthetic Guide (“Catalyst,” “the app”) is an educational application intended to provide general information related to lower-limb prosthetics, common troubleshooting topics, and peer support resources.

Information We Collect

Catalyst does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share personal data.

  • No user accounts or logins
  • No analytics or advertising services
  • No tracking for advertising purposes
  • No precise GPS location collection by Catalyst

Information Stored on Your Device

Some app preferences and selections (for example, favorites or onboarding selections) may be stored locally on your device to improve your experience. This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to the developer.

ZIP Code Search and Maps

If you use the peer support group search feature, you may enter a ZIP code to filter results. The ZIP code is used within the app to display nearby listings and is not stored, retained, or transmitted by Catalyst.

The map feature may use Apple Maps to render map tiles and pins. Apple’s services may process information as part of providing maps functionality. Please review Apple’s privacy practices for additional details.

Email Links

If you choose to contact a listed organization or contact the developer, Catalyst may open your device’s default email application. Catalyst does not collect, access, or store the contents of emails you send.

Medical Disclaimer

Catalyst is intended for educational purposes only and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a licensed healthcare professional for medical decisions.

Children’s Privacy

Catalyst does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information to the developer through email, please contact us so we can address it.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.
