Privacy Policy – Catalyst: Prosthetic Guide

Last updated: January 2026

Catalyst: Prosthetic Guide (“Catalyst,” “the app”) is an educational application intended to provide general information and guidance related to lower-limb prosthetics.

Information Collection and Use

Catalyst does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

  • No user accounts or login
  • No analytics or advertising services
  • No location tracking

Any preferences or selections are stored locally on the user’s device.

Medical Disclaimer

Catalyst is intended for educational purposes only and does not provide medical advice. Users should consult a licensed healthcare professional for medical decisions.

Children’s Privacy

Catalyst does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
